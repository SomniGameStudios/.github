# SomniGameStudios

We're building **Stepland** --- a gamified healthy habits mobile game
where your real-world physical activity brings a virtual village to
life.

Built with the Godot Engine for Android and iOS.

## ðŸ”Œ Mobile Integrations

Shipping a real mobile game means real mobile infrastructure.

Here's what powers Stepland:

-   **Backend** --- Firebase + PlayFab\
-   **Authentication** --- Firebase\
-   **Push Notifications** --- Firebase Cloud Messaging\
-   **IAPs & Subscriptions** --- Google Play Billing (Android),
    StoreKit2 (iOS), RevenueCat\
-   **In-App Ads** --- AdMob\
-   **Crash Reporting** --- Sentry (planned)\
-   **Attribution / MMP** --- TBD\
-   **Social Features** --- TBD\
-   **Analytics** --- ByteBrew\
-   **Health Data** --- Custom Android plugin + HealthKit (iOS)\
-   **Testing** --- GdUnit4\
-   **CI/CD** --- GitHub Actions

Many of the bridges between Godot and these native services are being
developed right here.
